RULES OF THE QUIZ

  1. Anyone suspected by an official of cheating will be disqualified. You must not use any mobile device during a quiz competition.
  2. If a player arrives after a competition has begun, they will not be allowed into the quiz room as it could disrupt other players.
  3. For each question answered correctly by the participants, a +3 points will be awarded to the team.
  4. If a team fail to provide an answer to a question, it will be forwarded to the opposing team. A +2 points will be awarded to the team.
  5. If both teams can not provide an answer, the question will be forwarded to the audience. If answered correctly, a +1 point will be awarded to the team.
  6. Failure to abide by any of the stated rules, either by the participants or their supporters will attract a -2 points to the responsible team.
